API Usage

  • Background
    • User's information, status, cooldown, money, events, messages and refills. (every 30 seconds (configurable))

    • Faction crime data. (every 15 minutes)
    • Torn's stock data. (every 15 minutes, synced with the stock ticks)
    • Torn's items, point values, honors, medals and educations. (daily)
  • Individually
    • Live Networth - 1 request
  • Cache
    • Live Networth - 5 minutes
